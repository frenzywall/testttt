How It Works

1. Email Upload

Upload your .msg format email containing maintenance window information.

2. AI Processing

Our AI model analyzes the email content to identify:

  • Affected services
  • Maintenance windows
  • Impact assessments

3. Time Conversion

Convert maintenance windows between Sweden time (CET/CEST) and IST with our built-in converter.

4. Review & Export

Review the processed information, make any necessary edits, and export as HTML for sharing.
